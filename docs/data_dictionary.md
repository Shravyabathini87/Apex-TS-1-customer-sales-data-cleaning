# Data Dictionary

CustomerID — Unique identifier for each customer (Integer)

Name — Customer full name (String)

DateOfBirth — Customer birth date (Date)

Gender — Gender of customer (Categorical)

Email — Customer email address (String)

TransactionID — Unique transaction identifier (String)

TransactionDate — Date of purchase (Date)

Product — Product purchased (Categorical)

Amount — Purchase amount in INR (Numeric)

City — Customer city (Categorical)

CustomerAge — Age calculated from DateOfBirth (Numeric)

ProductCategory — Derived category of product (Electronics/Accessories)
